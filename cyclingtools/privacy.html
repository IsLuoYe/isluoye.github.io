3. Permission Usage

3.1 Camera Permission

This app only requests camera permission to control the device's flashlight functionality. We do not use the camera for taking photos, recording videos, or capturing any image or video data. You can revoke this permission at any time in your device settings. After revoking the permission, flashlight functionality will be unavailable, but other features will not be affected.
